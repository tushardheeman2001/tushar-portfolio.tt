About Me

Hi, I'm Tushar Dheeman — a passionate graphic designer and UX/UI enthusiast from New Delhi. My top tools include Figma, Photoshop, CorelDRAW, and Canva, and I'm steadily building strong skills in HTML & CSS.

I specialize in crafting bold branding, clean user interfaces, and engaging motion designs that elevate the user experience. I’m currently a Design Instructor at Nishkam Institute and love teaching creative minds.

My mission is to turn ideas into visual impact — whether it's through web interfaces, motion storytelling, or seamless design systems. I’m exploring deeper into web design and love merging aesthetics with usability.
